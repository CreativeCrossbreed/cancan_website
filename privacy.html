Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction

Can Can provides a digital solution for drinking water can delivery in India. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information (name, phone, email) provided when placing orders.
  • Order details (brand, quantity, delivery address, timeslot).
  • Payment status and transaction references when provided by vendors.
  • Usage data and device information when using vendor or admin apps.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to process orders, assign vendors, manage deliveries, and for customer support. We may also use anonymized data for analytics and service improvements.

WhatsApp Ordering

When you place orders via WhatsApp, messages and responses are processed to extract order details. We recommend not sharing sensitive personal data over messaging platforms.

Data Sharing

We share necessary order information with assigned vendors to fulfil deliveries. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Security & Retention

We take reasonable measures to protect data. We retain personal information as long as needed to provide services and comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction or deletion of personal data by contacting us at admin@cancanindia.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email admin@cancanindia.com.
